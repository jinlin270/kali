CORNELL KALI CLUB

About Us

We are a diverse community dedicated to spreading cultural awareness and cultivate strength through trainings of kali at Cornell University. We host weekly trainings with certified and experienced trainers who are always on-hand and ready to help. We also host seminars every semester featuring grand masters from different schools of Filipino Martial Arts to enrich our understanding of the diverse culture. All of our events are beginner friendly, and all are welcome to join.
